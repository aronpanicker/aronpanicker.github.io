chicken biriyani

ingredients

500 g chicken, bone-in
2 cups basmati rice
2 pcs onions, sliced
2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
1 cup yogurt
2 tbsp biriyani masala

method

  1. soak rice for 20 to 30 minutes.
  2. marinate chicken with yogurt and spices.
  3. cook chicken, parboil rice, then dum-cook layered pot.

minimal by design. 1mb club mindset.
